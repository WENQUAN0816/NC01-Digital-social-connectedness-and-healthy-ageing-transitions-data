# 论文故事线与创新性定位

## 一句话主线

本研究不是简单回答“互联网使用或社会参与是否有益健康”，而是提出：在快速老龄化和数字化并行的社会背景下，数字连接和现实社会参与可能共同构成老年人健康老龄化的两类 connectedness resources，并影响其后续健康老龄化状态转移。

## 推荐论文题目

### 稳健版

Digital and social connectedness trajectories and healthy ageing transitions across harmonized ageing cohorts

### 更像 Nature Aging / NC 的版本

Digital-social connectedness and multidimensional healthy ageing trajectories across international ageing cohorts

### 中文理解

跨国老龄化队列中数字-社会连接与多维健康老龄化轨迹

## 核心科学问题

既往研究多把互联网使用、社会参与、认知、衰弱或抑郁分开研究，也常局限于单一国家或单一健康域。本研究要回答的是：

1. 数字连接和社会参与是否分别与后续健康老龄化相关？
2. 同时具备数字连接和社会参与的人群，是否表现出最有利的健康老龄化状态转移？
3. 数字/社会参与的动态变化，例如 adopted、persistent、stopped，是否比静态暴露更能解释健康老龄化轨迹？
4. 这些关联是否在不同国家队列、年龄、性别、教育、城乡和基线健康状态中一致？
5. 认知、心理健康和功能状态是否提示潜在路径？

## 最重要的创新点

### 1. 从单一暴露转向 dual connectedness resources

传统研究通常单独分析 internet use 或 social participation。本研究把二者放在同一个概念框架中，定义为：

- digital connectedness: internet use
- social connectedness: social participation
- joint connectedness: neither / internet only / social only / both

创新性表述：

> We conceptualized digital inclusion and social participation as two complementary connectedness resources in later life, rather than treating them as isolated behaviours.

### 2. 从单一健康结局转向 multidimensional healthy ageing

既往研究常看 cognition、frailty、depression 或 ADL 等单一健康域。本研究构建多维 healthy ageing 指标，并进一步拆解健康域。

创新性表述：

> By using a multidimensional healthy ageing construct, this study moves beyond single-domain outcomes and evaluates whether connectedness is associated with broader ageing trajectories.

### 3. 从静态相关转向 healthy ageing transitions

本研究不只是看某一波是否健康，而是看：

- unhealthy -> healthy: gain healthy ageing
- healthy -> healthy: maintain healthy ageing
- healthy/unhealthy 状态随下一波变化

关键结果：

- both digital + social connectedness 与 gain healthy ageing 相关：OR = 1.68, 95% CI 1.37-2.06
- both digital + social connectedness 与 maintain healthy ageing 相关：OR = 1.65, 95% CI 1.30-2.10

创新性表述：

> The trajectory-based design allowed us to distinguish between maintaining healthy ageing and gaining healthy ageing, which cannot be captured in conventional cross-sectional analyses.

### 4. 从是否使用转向 adoption / persistence / stopping

数字包容不是静态状态。你的结果显示：

- adopted internet use: OR = 1.24
- persistent internet use: OR = 1.51
- stopped internet use: OR = 1.09, not significant

这可以成为非常强的故事点：持续数字连接比短期或停止连接更稳定地关联健康老龄化。

创新性表述：

> Persistent digital engagement, rather than transient or discontinued use, was most consistently associated with favourable healthy ageing transitions.

### 5. 跨国 harmonized ageing cohorts

本研究使用 HRS-family harmonized ageing cohorts，覆盖 CHARLS、ELSA、HRS、KLoSA、MHAS、SHARE，并根据变量可比性决定不同分析纳入队列。

创新性表述：

> The harmonized multi-cohort design provides cross-national evidence while respecting cohort-specific measurement availability, rather than forcing non-comparable variables into a pooled analysis.

## 核心发现怎么讲

### 发现 1：数字和社会连接同时存在的人群健康老龄化优势最大

主结果：

- both vs neither: OR = 1.65, 95% CI 1.36-2.00
- internet only vs neither: OR = 1.49, 95% CI 1.44-1.55
- social only vs neither: OR = 1.15, 95% CI 1.01-1.31

推荐写法：

> Older adults with both digital and social connectedness had the highest odds of subsequent healthy ageing compared with those with neither form of connectedness.

注意边界：

不能写“协同效应已经被证明”。因为：

- multiplicative interaction OR = 1.00
- RERI = 0.07, 95% CI -0.07 to 0.20

稳健写法：

> Although formal evidence for multiplicative or additive interaction was limited, the joint-exposure group consistently showed the most favourable healthy ageing profile.

### 发现 2：持续互联网使用比新采用或停止使用更有意义

结果：

- persistent internet use: OR = 1.51
- adopted internet use: OR = 1.24
- stopped internet use: OR = 1.09, not significant

推荐写法：

> The strongest association was observed among persistent internet users, whereas discontinued use was not clearly associated with subsequent healthy ageing.

中文解释：

这说明论文不只是“上网有益”，而是“持续数字参与可能是健康老龄化轨迹中的稳定资源”。

### 发现 3：结果体现在健康维持和健康获得两个方向

结果：

- gain healthy: both OR = 1.68
- maintain healthy: both OR = 1.65

推荐写法：

> Connectedness was associated not only with maintaining healthy ageing among those already healthy, but also with gaining healthy ageing among those initially unhealthy.

这比单纯 prevalence 更有故事性。

### 发现 4：机制线索集中在认知、心理健康和功能状态

健康域结果：

- internet use -> cognition_not_low: OR = 2.50
- internet use -> no ADL limitation: OR = 1.53
- internet use -> not depressive: OR = 1.39
- social participation -> cognition_not_low: OR = 1.35
- social participation -> no ADL limitation: OR = 1.32
- social participation -> not depressive: OR = 1.19

推荐写法：

> Domain-specific analyses suggested that cognitive, psychological, and functional health domains may be particularly relevant to the observed associations.

注意边界：

不要写成 confirmed mediation。应写 potential pathways / mechanistic clues。

## Introduction 逻辑

### 第一段：社会背景

全球老龄化和数字化同时发生。互联网、数字服务、远程医疗和线上社会连接正在改变老年人的生活资源结构。但数字鸿沟也可能扩大健康不平等。

### 第二段：已有研究

已有研究提示互联网使用、社会参与与认知、抑郁、衰弱、功能状态有关。但多数研究存在三个限制：

1. 单独研究数字使用或社会参与，缺少联合 connectedness 框架。
2. 聚焦单一健康域，而非 multidimensional healthy ageing。
3. 多为单一国家或横断面/静态暴露，较少考察动态轨迹和健康状态转移。

### 第三段：本研究填补空白

本研究使用 harmonized international ageing cohorts，构建 digital-social connectedness 框架，考察其与下一波 healthy ageing、健康状态转移、暴露动态变化和健康域分解的关联。

### 第四段：假设

我们假设：

1. digital connectedness 和 social connectedness 均与后续 healthy ageing 正相关。
2. 同时具备两类 connectedness 的人群表现出最有利的 healthy ageing transitions。
3. persistent connectedness 比 adopted 或 stopped connectedness 更稳定地关联健康老龄化。
4. 这些关联可能主要反映认知、心理健康和功能状态相关路径。

## Abstract 结果段模板

> Across harmonized international ageing cohorts, older adults with both digital and social connectedness had higher odds of subsequent healthy ageing than those with neither form of connectedness. This pattern was observed for both gaining and maintaining healthy ageing. Persistent internet use showed a stronger association than newly adopted use, whereas discontinued use was not clearly associated with subsequent healthy ageing. The findings were robust to inverse probability weighting for attrition and alternative definitions of healthy ageing. Domain-specific analyses suggested that cognitive, psychological, and functional health domains may contribute to these associations.

## Discussion 第一段模板

> In this harmonized multi-cohort study of older adults, digital and social connectedness were associated with favourable healthy ageing transitions. The most favourable profile was observed among individuals with both forms of connectedness, and the association was evident for both maintaining healthy ageing and gaining healthy ageing over follow-up. Importantly, persistent digital engagement was more strongly associated with healthy ageing than newly adopted or discontinued use, suggesting that sustained connectedness may be a relevant marker of healthy ageing trajectories in later life.

## 最适合投稿时强调的 novelty claim

不要写：

> This is the first study to examine internet use and healthy ageing.

建议写：

> To our knowledge, this is among the first harmonized multi-cohort studies to examine digital and social connectedness as joint and dynamic resources for multidimensional healthy ageing transitions.

更强但仍然稳健的写法：

> This study extends prior work by shifting the focus from isolated digital behaviours or single health domains to dynamic digital-social connectedness patterns and multidimensional healthy ageing transitions across international ageing cohorts.

## 论文卖点排序

1. Cross-national harmonized ageing cohorts
2. Digital-social connectedness framework
3. Joint exposure: both connectedness highest
4. Dynamic exposure: persistent/adopted/stopped
5. Healthy ageing transitions: gain and maintain
6. Robustness: IPW, alternative definitions, leave-one-cohort-out
7. Mechanistic clues: cognition, depression, function

## 审稿人可能质疑及应对

### 质疑 1：这不是因果研究

回应：

本研究采用 lagged longitudinal design，控制 baseline healthy ageing，并进行 IPW 和多种敏感性分析。我们避免因果措辞，使用 associated with。

### 质疑 2：异质性太高

回应：

异质性本身是跨国 ageing cohorts 的重要发现。我们使用 random-effects meta-analysis、leave-one-cohort-out 和 subgroup analyses 来描述和解释异质性。

### 质疑 3：healthy ageing 指标是否人为构造

回应：

我们使用多种定义进行敏感性分析，包括严格定义、宽松定义和去认知组件定义，结果方向一致。

### 质疑 4：机制分析是否循环

回应：

健康域分析只作为 domain-specific pattern 和 mechanistic clues，不作为正式中介因果结论。

## 最终故事版本

这篇文章的最佳故事不是“互联网让老人更健康”，而是：

在全球老龄化和数字化并行的背景下，老年人的健康老龄化不仅取决于传统人口学和健康因素，也与其是否持续嵌入数字和现实社会连接网络有关。基于多个 harmonized ageing cohorts，本研究发现数字连接和社会参与共同定义了不同的 connectedness profiles；同时具备两类连接的人群最可能维持或获得健康老龄化，而持续互联网使用比新采用或停止使用更稳定地关联有利轨迹。这些关联在多种稳健性分析中保持一致，并主要体现在认知、心理健康和功能状态相关健康域上。研究将既往关于数字使用或社会参与的分散证据，推进为一个跨国、动态、多维健康老龄化的 connectedness framework。
